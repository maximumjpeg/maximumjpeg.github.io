Mushihimesama is a great Danmaku game!

Another banger from Cave.

Riding a flying beetle that shoots lasers, Insect Princess Reco must battle other laser firing insects.

Mushihimesama is an underground legend of an arcade game. Amongst the many hyperfast shoot 'em up Danmaku (lit. Bullet Hell) games from Japan, this one stands out in particular as something very special.

This game has maintained a cult following in the community. Adored by the diehard community of Danmaku enthusiasts. Albeit a very tough love, for the genre's games are famous for being brutally difficult due to complex bullet spread patterns that require lightning fast reflexes to survive. A common mechanic in Danmaku is being able to slow your movement speed down when you want to glide your character through a maze of enemy fire. The majority of these games are 1 hit = 1 life in terms of player health and lives. As you can imagine, it can be very stressing to manage through the curtain fire constantly raining down the screen.
